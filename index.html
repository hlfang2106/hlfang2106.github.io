About me

I am a tenure-track Assistant Professor in the School of Mathematical Sciences at Peking University. Previously, I was a Van Vleck Visiting Assistant Professor at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. I received my PhD (2018) from Rutgers University–New Brunswick. and Bachelor degree (2012) from Tsinghua University.

Research interest

My current interests are the spectral theory on arithmetic Riemann surfaces and the complex geometry motivated by quantum gravity.
